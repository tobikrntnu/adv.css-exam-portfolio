Garmin Vivoactive 5

Key Features

Weather

You can check the weather directly on your smart watch. Get additional information such as UV index, air humidity and cloud cover.

Messages

Receive and send text messages directly from the watch

Activities

Start and perform different forms of exercise and track them with the new technology used to precisely show your activity level.

Body battery

Get an insight into your energy levels throughout the day. Learn when you need to rest, or when you can push on.

Specifications

Feature Details
Compatibility Compatible with both Apple and Android
Sensors Heart rate, Pulse oximeter, Accelerometer & Compass
Material Reinforced polymer case with silicone straps
Display 1,2" AMOLED, 390x390 pixels, touchscreen
Battery life Up to 11 days battery life
Water resistance Water resistant (made for swimming)
Training modes Running, cycling, strength, yoga, HIIT, specialized sport programs

Fitness tracking

Track steps

Track your steps and set goals to motivate you to become more active!

VO2 max

See your current max oxygen intake to check your current fitness level

Intensity minutes

Track the amount of active minutes you have during a week

Fitness age

Gives you an overall health and fitness level compared to your biological age

Total calories burnt

Check how many calories you burn both inactively during the day, and from exercise

Recovery

Get a detailed time to restitute based on your previous activity
