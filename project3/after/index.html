Facts about t-shirts and sustainability

  • 🌊 Producing one T-shirt requires about 2,700 liters of water.
  • 🌱 Cotton farming uses a lot of water and pesticides.
  • 🚛 Transportation and production account for around 8% of global emissions.

Tips for smarter clothing choices!

  • 🧵 Choose quality and wear your clothes longer.
  • 🔧 Repair instead of throwing away.
  • 🌿 Opt for organic or recycled materials.

End of life: What happens with your T-shirt?

  • ♻️ Recycle: Check local textile recycling programs to reduce landfill waste.
  • 🤝 Donate or Sell: Give your t-shirt a second chance with someone else.
  • 🛠️ Upcycle: Turn your old shirts into rags, bags, or new creations.
  • 🚫 Avoid Waste: Don’t throw clothes away — it harms the environment.
  • 🌿 Choose Recyclable Materials: Natural fibers like organic cotton biodegrade better.
